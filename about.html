Personal Information

My name is Cameron Hall. I am a second year student at Michigan State University studying my passion - film production. When I was a child, I was fascinated by the magic in films like Star Wars and Harry Potter. I vowed that, someday, I would make movies. Since then, that has been my goal. I now write, film, edit, and produce short films, and I have much prepared for the future. However, there is much more to me. I am also a fan of European football (soccer), and I am a huge fan of Liverpool (England), Juventus (Italy), Real Oveido (Spain), and Tokyo Verdy (Japan). I played the sport for 15 years, and it became my life. With this, I have a passion for fitness, and I love learning about and studying the muscular system of humans to better understand how the body responds to working out. As for music, I spent a year or two playing the baritone, but found my footing in percussion, specializing in the marimba and the timpani. About two years ago, I delved into vocals and actually was Davey (a main role) in a production of Newsies. Before this production, I spent most of my theatre days in the tech booth as the student head of sound. It was here that I found my passion for sound technology that takes a part in how I create and edit films today. At the end of the day, I am working to become the best filmmaker I can be and use my many passions as a source of inspiration as I create my journey forward.
